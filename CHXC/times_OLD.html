About:

~~~~~~~~~~

This site shows statistics and performances for Chapel Hill High School Cross Country. Over time we will add more and more historical performances.

Can't you just get this data from Milesplit? Some of it you certainly can. Here's how it's different:

  • We include 1 mile and 2 mile splits when we can capture them so we can calculate negative splits.
  • Over time we will include performances from major workouts that we do each year (mini-michigan, mile time trials, Watauga Road, etc).
  • No Milesplit subscription is required, so CHHS parents can view kids' performances for free.
  • All time CHHS performance lists by race, by course, and by workout.

NOTE: Data is up to date for current team members. There is a good deal of historical data from older teams but it is in no way complete.
